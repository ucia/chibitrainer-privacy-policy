This Privacy Policy explains how Chibi Trainer (“the App”) handles information. Chibi Trainer is published by Ucia.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not require account creation and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, microphone recordings, or precise location.

The App stores gameplay-related progress locally on your device, such as game progress, settings, and similar save data.

The App may display rewarded advertisements through third-party advertising and mediation services, including Unity LevelPlay / ironSource. These services may process information such as advertising identifiers, IP address, app-specific identifiers, technical device and app information, and ad interaction information in order to deliver ads, support reporting, prevent fraud, and manage ad frequency.

2. How Information Is Used

  • To save and restore gameplay progress and settings on your device.
  • To provide rewarded ads and related ad mediation functionality.
  • To support ad delivery, fraud prevention, reporting, and frequency capping by advertising partners.
  • To operate the app on Android and Google Play services where applicable.

3. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell your personal information. Data may be processed by third-party service providers used by the App, such as Unity LevelPlay / ironSource and other advertising partners involved in ad mediation and rewarded ads.

4. Data Storage and Retention

Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device and remain there until you remove the app data, uninstall the app, or clear local storage.

Data processed by third-party advertising or platform services is handled according to their own retention and privacy practices.

5. Security

The developer takes reasonable steps to avoid unnecessary collection of personal data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

6. Children

Chibi Trainer is intended for users aged 13 and older. The App is not intended for children under 13.

7. Your Choices

  • You can stop all local data storage by uninstalling the App or clearing its app data from your device settings.
  • You can limit advertising identifier use through your Android device privacy and advertising settings.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as:

  • Google Play / Android platform services
  • Unity LevelPlay / ironSource for rewarded ads and mediation
